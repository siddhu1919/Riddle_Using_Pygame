1. Riddle: I speak without a mouth and hear without ears. I have no body, but I come alive with the wind. What am I?
   Answer: An echo.

2. Riddle: The more you take, the more you leave behind. What am I?
   Answer: Footsteps.

3. Riddle: I am taken from a mine, and shut up in a wooden case, from which I am never released, and yet I am used by almost every person. What am I?
   Answer: Pencil lead/graphite.

4. Riddle: I have keys but open no locks. I have space but no room. You can enter, but you can't go inside. What am I?
   Answer: A keyboard.

5. Riddle: The person who makes it, sells it. The person who buys it never uses it. What is it?
   Answer: A coffin.

6. Riddle: I can fly without wings. I can cry without eyes. Wherever I go, darkness follows me. What am I?
   Answer: Cloud.
